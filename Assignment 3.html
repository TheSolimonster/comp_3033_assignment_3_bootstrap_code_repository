About Me

Hello and welcome to Khalid Soliman's Portfolio. My name is Khalid Soliman, and I am 22 years old. I am currently in my 5th and final year at Acadia University, and I was born on November 2nd, 2002. I have four family members, excluding myself, and I am the youngest of three brothers. I am originally from Alexandria, Egypt, but I was born in Saudi Arabia and lived there until I turned 9. My family and I moved to Toronto, Canada, where I lived until I was 13. Afterward, I moved to Abu Dhabi, UAE, where I stayed until the age of 17, and finally settled in Wolfville, Nova Scotia, in 2020.

Outside of school, I spend my time pursuing hobbies I enjoy. Some of these hobbies include playing football as an Offensive Lineman for Acadia University, playing video games, lifting weights, going on walks, and listening to music. I am proficient in two languages: English and Arabic. Additionally, I have beginner-level skills in two other languages: German and French. I take pride in stepping outside my comfort zone, and I am passionate about working hard to achieve my goals.

I aim to understand the world of full-stack cloud computing in this course. I have never built a website before, as I only began learning about computer science after I started at Acadia University. I am eager to learn everything this course has to offer and dive deeper into a software developer's mindset. I am also excited to complete my academic journey at Acadia University, secure my Bachelor of Computer Science, and make my family and friends proud.
